### Text style

  - Bold, *Italic*, Underline, Strikethrough
  - Subscript and Superscript 
  - Font color, Font background color
  - Font select, Font size
  
### Formatting

  - List : OL/UL 
  - Alignment(Left/Center/Right) 
  - Indent(increase/decrease) 
  - Image float left/right
  - Hyper Link
  - Blockquote
  
### Miscellaneous

  - Image resizing/move
  - Drag & Drop selection
  - Copy & Paste, Cut
  - Smart scaling
  - Zoom & Scaling
  - Undo/Redo
